Mechanical Team

What We Do

The Mechanical Team is responsible for designing, building, and optimizing every physical component of our sailboat.

Within mech team there are many sub-teams where you can take ownership over part of the boat. Prior projects have included making the hull, mast, rudder, sails, crows nest and interior controls mounting.

Skills You'll Develop

  • CAD - Fusion360, Solidworks
  • Physics Simulation - Orca3D, Ansys
  • Manufacturing - Fiberglass, 3D printing, laser cutting
  • Quality Control - Measurement, testing & validation

No prior experience required! We welcome students from all engineering disciplines and skill levels.
