Do I need prior experience to join?

Not at all! We welcome students from all disciplines and skill levels. We provide training and mentorship to help you learn.

How much time commitment is expected?

We recommend attending our two weekly meetings. If you can only make one that is also ok! We'll adapt the scope of your projects to fit your availability. Participation is completely voluntary, and the more active you are the more you'll get to do!

Can I join mid-semester?

Absolutely! We accept new members throughout the academic year. Just reach out to us on Discord or email and we'll help you get started.

Are there any dues?

Our club is completely free to join. Members are only expected to pay for a portion of travel expenses if choosing to attend competition.
