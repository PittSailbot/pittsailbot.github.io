What Sailbots about

SailBot is a project-based club that aims to compete in the annual International Robotic Sailing Competition. The sailboat will be tested in numerous aspects of its design including autonomous navigation, speed, pathfinding and endurance. Over the course of a year we are tasked with designing, programming and fabricating this autonomous sailbot, all while gaining experience from other students and learning new skills you won't find in the classroom.
